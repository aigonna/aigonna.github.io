Ap1=λ1p1⋮Apn=λnpn\begin{aligned} \boldsymbol{A} \boldsymbol{p}_{1} &=\lambda_{1} \boldsymbol{p}_{1} \\ & \vdots \\ \boldsymbol{A} \boldsymbol{p}_{n} &=\lambda_{n} \boldsymbol{p}_{n} \end{aligned} Ap1​Apn​​=λ1​p1​⋮=λn​pn​​
