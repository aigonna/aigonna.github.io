Why can’t we set w^=X−1y\hat w = X^{-1}yw^=X−1y?

  • X−1X^{-1}X−1does not exist。

When w^=(XTX)−1XTy\hat w = (X^TX)^{-1}X^Tyw^=(XTX)−1XTy vs w^=XT(XXT)−1y\hat w = X^T(XX^T)^{-1}yw^=XT(XXT)−1y？

  • w^=(XTX)−1XTy\hat w = (X^TX)^{-1}X^Tyw^=(XTX)−1XTy : $n \ge p, \ X \ has \ p \ LI \ cols. $
